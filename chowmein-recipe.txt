Ingredients
Chop suey noodles
1 Red Pepper
Beansprouts
Chicken breast
Sesame oil
Soy sauce
Mirin
Corn flour
Salt
Sugar

Directions
1. Soak the noodles for 8mins
2. Slice chicken breast and marinade with soy sauce and corn flour
3. Julienne the pepper
4. In a pan at medium high heat fry the chicken until brown then add the peppers and beansprouts
5. Add seasoning and cook till veg softens
6. Drain the noodles and add to pan
7. Stir fry together until well mixed
